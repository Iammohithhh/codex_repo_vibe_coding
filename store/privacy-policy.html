Privacy Policy for Paper2Product

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Paper2Product, an AI Research Operating System that transforms research papers into production-ready code and assets. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Account Information

When you create an account with Paper2Product, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username and password (encrypted)
  • Name and profile information (optional)
  • Account creation date and authentication data

2.2 Research Paper Content

When you upload research papers to the App for processing, we collect and store:

  • The full text and metadata of uploaded papers
  • Generated code and assets derived from your papers
  • Your editing history and modifications
  • File formats and document properties

2.3 Usage Analytics

We collect non-personally identifiable information about how you use the App:

  • Features accessed and frequency of use
  • Time spent on different features
  • Device type, operating system version, and mobile network information
  • Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Aggregate anonymized usage patterns

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

Your device may automatically provide us with:

  • Device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • IP address and location information (if permitted)
  • Log data and session timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

Paper2Product uses collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our AI Research OS features
  • To process your research papers and generate production-ready code
  • To authenticate your account and prevent fraud
  • To communicate with you about updates, features, and support
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize the App experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • To provide customer support and technical assistance
  • To send periodic newsletters and promotional communications (with your consent)

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 What We Do NOT Do

Paper2Product does NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information or research data to any third party.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App and providing services (e.g., cloud storage providers, analytics services). These providers are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request, we may disclose information. We will notify you of such requests when legally permitted to do so.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users of any such change.
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information with specific third parties.

5. Data Security

Paper2Product implements comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL protocols and at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure, access-controlled servers with regular security audits and monitoring.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your personal information on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular Updates: We maintain and update our security infrastructure to protect against emerging threats.
  • Password Protection: User passwords are hashed and salted using industry-standard algorithms.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 90 days after deletion/deactivation.
  • Research Papers: Retained as long as you maintain an active account. Upon account deletion, papers are securely deleted within 30 days.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized usage data is retained for up to 2 years for service improvement.
  • Legal Data: Information may be retained longer if required by law or legal proceedings.

7. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. You can update your account settings directly in the App or contact us for assistance.

7.2 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We will delete your information within 30 days, except where legally required to retain it.

7.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in our emails or adjusting your notification preferences in the App.

7.4 Location and Permissions

You can control location sharing and other device permissions through your device settings. Disabling permissions may limit some App functionality.

7.5 Regional Rights

If you are in the EU, California, or other jurisdictions with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights including GDPR rights, CCPA rights, or equivalent protections. Please contact us for more information.

8. Children's Privacy

Paper2Product is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through the App. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing your information.
